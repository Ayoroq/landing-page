This website is about birds

Birds are fascinating creatures found all over the world. They come in a dazzling variety of shapes, sizes, and colors, and play important roles in ecosystems as pollinators, seed dispersers, and indicators of environmental health.

Example birds for highlight

Cockatiels are small, social parrots known for their friendly and playful nature. Popular pets due small size, and ability to mimic human speech.

The Brahminy Kite (Haliastur indus) is a medium-sized bird of prey with distinctive reddish-brown plumage and a white head and chest

The common wood pigeon are known for their grey and lilac plumage, white neck patches, and distinctive white wing bands visible in flight.

The Greater Flamingo are known for their long legs, long neck, and distinctive pinkish-white plumage with red wing coverts and black flight feathers.
